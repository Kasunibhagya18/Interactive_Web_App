Typing Tips & Techniques

Master these techniques to become a typing pro

Proper Hand Position

Keep your fingers on the home row (ASDF JKL;). Your index fingers should rest on F and J keys which have small bumps.

Don't Look at Keyboard

Train yourself to type without looking at the keys. This improves speed and helps develop muscle memory.

Start Slow

Accuracy is more important than speed. Start slow and gradually increase your pace as you improve.

Focus on Accuracy

Maintain high accuracy (95%+) rather than rushing. Speed naturally improves with consistent practice.

Practice Daily

Regular practice is key. Even 15-20 minutes daily can significantly improve your typing skills.

Set Goals

Track your progress and set achievable goals. Celebrate small improvements along the way.

Pro Tips

Good Posture

Sit up straight with your feet flat on the floor. Keep your wrists slightly elevated and avoid resting them on the desk while typing.

Take Breaks

Rest your hands every 30 minutes. Stretch your fingers and wrists to prevent strain and fatigue.

Use All Fingers

Distribute the work across all ten fingers. Each finger has specific keys it should press for optimal efficiency.

Learn Shortcuts

Master keyboard shortcuts for common actions. This saves time and reduces mouse usage.
